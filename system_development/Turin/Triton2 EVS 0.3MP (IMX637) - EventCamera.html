Triton2 EVS 0.3 MP Model (IMX637)

  1. Home
  2. Triton2 EVS 2.5GigE Camera
  3. Triton2 EVS – 0.3 MP Sony | Prophesee IMX637 CMOS Triton2 EVS 0.9 MP
The Triton2 EVS camera featuring the event-based IMX637, delivers low latency event-based data. This innovative approach involves event-based pixels that are constantly sampling incoming light, generating signals only when there’s a change in light levels, rather than capturing traditional frames. The shutter-free sensor tracks illumination changes, responding with ON or OFF events for intensity shifts that surpasses set thresholds. The creation of the IMX636 and IMX637 sensors was a result of a successful collaboration between Sony and Prophesee, bringing together Sony’s expertise in CMOS image sensor technology and Prophesee’s distinctive event-based Metavision® sensing technologies.
